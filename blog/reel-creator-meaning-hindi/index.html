Reel Creator Ka Matlab Kya Hota Hai?

Instagram par reel creator ka matlab hota hai wo user account jo vertical short-form videos (Reels) banata aur share karta hai. Jab aap normal account se Professional Account ya Creator Account par switch karte hain, toh aapko extra tools (analytics, insights, direct DM actions) milte hain.

  • Reel Creator Profile Layout: Aapki profile par ek specific category title show hota hai (jaise Video Creator, Digital Creator, etc.).
  • Reel Creator ka Matlab Hindi Mein: Reels banane wala vyakti jo organic views, templates aur trending music features ka use karke views gain karta hai.

---

Creator Account Kaise Setup Kare?

Agar aap Instagram par professional reel creator banana chahte hain, toh in steps ko follow karein:

1. Apne Instagram Profile par jayein aur Top-Right menu par click karein.

2. Settings and Privacy par click karke scroll karein aur 'Account Type and Tools' select karein.

3. 'Switch to Professional Account' par tap karein aur 'Creator' option select karein.

4. Category mein 'Digital Creator' ya 'Video Creator' select karein.
