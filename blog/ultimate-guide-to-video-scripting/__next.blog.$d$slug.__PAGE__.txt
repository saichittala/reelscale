10:T545,<h2 id="introduction">Introduction</h2>
<p>Even the most beautiful cinematic footage will fail if the script is weak. A high-retention Reel is built on clear pacing, psychological hooks, and value-first scripting. Learning the frameworks of <strong>video scripting for engagement</strong> is essential for creating consistent social media results.</p>
<p>---</p>
<h2 id="the-hook-story-offer-script-structure">The Hook-Story-Offer Script Structure</h2>
<p>To ensure your script holds attention, structure it using our three-step formula:</p>
<p>1.  <strong>The Hook (0 - 2 seconds):</strong> Call out your target audience's specific problem or promise a clear result.</p>
<p>2.  <strong>The Story/Body (2 - 25 seconds):</strong> Break down the solution into simple, concise points. Keep sentences under 8 words to make reading subtitles effortless.</p>
<p>3.  <strong>The Offer/CTA (25 - 30 seconds):</strong> Instruct the viewer exactly what to do next. Stick to a single action, such as commenting a keyword.</p>
<p>---</p>
<h2 id="editing-your-script-for-speech-pacing">Editing Your Script for Speech Pacing</h2>
<p>When writing your script, read it aloud. Cut any complex vocabulary, corporate jargon, or long sentences.
